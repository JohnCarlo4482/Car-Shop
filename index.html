Our Story

Founded in 2000 by automotive enthusiast John Mitchell, AutoDrive began as a small family-owned dealership with a simple vision: to provide exceptional vehicles and unmatched customer service.

Today, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted automotive retailers, having helped over 15,000 customers find their perfect vehicle. Our success is built on relationships, trust, and a genuine passion for automobiles.

We carefully curate our inventory to include only the finest vehicles from reputable manufacturers, ensuring every customer drives away with confidence and satisfaction.
